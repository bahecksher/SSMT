# Plan - Slick Character Voice
_Created: 2026-03-27 0020_

## Goal
Define Slick as a memorable in-world operator whose voice adds humor, warmth, and identity to the training-module framing without distracting from the arcade loop.

## Approach
1. Establish Slick's role in the game's fiction: retired salvager, practical mentor, operator of the training yard.
2. Lock in a few personality pillars so future writing stays consistent.
3. Keep "Slick" as the player-facing anchor, with optional fuller naming only if later UI or lore needs it.
4. Use short reactive lines for failure, success, extraction, and phase escalation instead of long dialogue.
5. Preserve tension by keeping callouts sparse, readable, and rooted in gameplay events.

## Scope boundaries
- No full narrative campaign
- No heavy lore or worldbuilding beyond what supports the training-module fantasy
- No implementation of dialogue systems yet
- No final rename decision beyond recommended options

## Open questions
- Should Slick remain only a voice/presence, or appear visually in menu/UI portrait form later?
- Should the game title stay long and in-universe, or be shortened for stronger store/browser readability?
- How often should reactive banter fire before it becomes noise?
