## Full notes

### Ship Destruction Debris
- New ShipDebris class: 5 wireframe fragments per destroyed ship
- Fragments inherit ship velocity + random scatter, spin, fade over 1.8s
- NPC = amber, Enemy = magenta, Player = cyan debris
- Debris also spawns when salvage depletes/expires (green/purple)

### Regent Antagonist
- RegentComm UI: red portrait at bottom of screen (opposite Slick at top)
- Angular aggressive face: narrow slit eyes, stern downturn mouth, red glow
- Three dialogue categories:
  - enemyEnter: threats on first enemy spawn (phase 5+)
  - beamUnlock: "big guns" on first beam (phase 7)
  - whyWontYouDie: escalating frustration every phase after 7
- regentLines.ts with same no-repeat selection as Slick

### Asteroid Bounce Rebalance
- Smaller-or-equal asteroid destroyed on collision with bigger one
- Equal-size asteroids: both destroyed
- All asteroids can split if large enough, otherwise shatter into debris
- Speed capped at 200px/s (DRIFTER_SPEED_MAX) to prevent runaway momentum
- No more infinite pinball — asteroids break apart quickly

### Proximity Mining
- Mining points now scale with distance: 1 pts/sec at outer edge, 15 pts/sec danger-close
- Quadratic curve so big payoff only when threading the needle
- Danger-close (>70% proximity) gets bigger/bolder floating text
- Beats standard salvage (10 pts/sec) when very close

### Invulnerability
- 2 seconds of invulnerability after countdown ends
- Player blinks to indicate invulnerable state
- No shield consumption during window

### Shield Drops + Rare Salvage
- Shield pickups from dead NPCs now inherit 50% of NPC velocity
- Rare salvage multiplier bumped: 4 + phase (was 2 + phase*0.5)
- Rare salvage lifetime extended to 12s (was 10s)
