## Full notes

New file: `src/game/ui/CustomCursor.ts`

- Hides native cursor via `scene.input.setDefaultCursor('none')` on construction; restores on destroy
- Skips entirely on touch devices (`scene.sys.game.device.input.touch`)
- Graphic uses `setScrollFactor(0)` + `setDepth(200)` so it stays screen-fixed and above everything (HologramOverlay is depth 100)
- Positioned each frame at `pointer.x / pointer.y` (screen coords)

Reticle design:
- Circle: 20px radius, white 0.4 alpha
- 4 tick marks at N/E/S/W: 4px gap + 6px length extending outward from circle
- Triangle: 7px radius (~66% of player's ~10.4px triR), same color/alpha

Angle logic:
- GameScene: `Math.atan2(pointer.worldY - player.y, pointer.worldX - player.x)` — points in direction of travel (FROM player TOWARD cursor)
- MenuScene: fixed at `-Math.PI / 2` (pointing up)
- Heading only updates when cursor is >8px from player (prevents jitter at close range)

Scene wiring (minimal, no other changes):
- GameScene: import, field, instantiate after HologramOverlay in create(), update() after hologramOverlay.update(), destroy() in cleanup()
- MenuScene: same pattern, no player arg passed to update()
