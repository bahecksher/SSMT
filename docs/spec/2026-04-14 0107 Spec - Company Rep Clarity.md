# Spec - Company Rep Clarity
_Created: 2026-04-14 0107_

## Goal
Make it obvious how players gain company reputation and when that reputation is actually awarded.

## MissionSelect clarity
- MissionSelect should explicitly state that accepted contracts pay bonus credits and company rep on extraction.
- Mission cards should reinforce that rep is tied to successful extraction.
- Locked favor cards should point players toward completing contracts to unlock company standing.

## Results clarity
- Extraction results should show the total company rep earned from completed contracts.
- Completed contract lines should show both the credit reward and the rep payout.
- Death results should make it clear that company rep was not claimed because extraction did not happen.

## Out of scope
- Rebalancing rep thresholds
- Changing mission generation or rewards
- Adding tutorials, modals, or new onboarding screens
