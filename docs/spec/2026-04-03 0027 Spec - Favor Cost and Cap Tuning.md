# Spec - Favor Cost and Cap Tuning
_Created: 2026-04-03 0027_

## Goal
Make liaison favors attainable earlier while preventing a full four-company boost stack from becoming the default pre-run loadout.

## Requirements
- Every MissionSelect favor costs `2000c`
- The player may arm at most two favors per deployment
- MissionSelect should clearly communicate when the two-favor cap has been reached
- Existing liaison identities, favor effects, and deploy-time wallet spending stay unchanged

## Out of scope
- Reworking favor effects or boost values
- Changing wallet payout percentages
- Adding favor cooldowns, unlock rules, or backend persistence changes
