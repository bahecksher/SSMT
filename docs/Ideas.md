# Ideas

## Salvage
    Randomize shape
    Should be spinning slowly
    Instead of existing indefinitely, give it a health bar that as it's "salvaged" decreases until 0 - should be larger enough to not be salvaged completeyl through unless it's on screen for a long time.
    Explodes on HP reaching 0 after a brief 3 second flashing period for player to escape.

## Asteroids
    Randomize shape
    Should be spinning slowly
    Split/Destroyed by laser
    Instead of existing indefinitely, give it a health bar that as it's "mined" decreases until 0


## Game State On Death
    Freeze Game State on death for 1 second in order to see where it touched
    Graphically - Invert the colors on death - Red background (flashes twice), black asteroids / salvage, White Ship

## Enemies
    Randomly spawn an enemy ship after phase 2 that tracks you
    Collidable with asteroids and either splits or destroys them
    If they touch the player without shields, player's ship is destroyed

## Lasers
    Have them begin at Phase 5

## Graphically
    Make the game look like you're piloting in a hologram
    Remake each of the assets in hologram style
    Hitboxes need to be clearly visible

## Slick
    Core role: retired salvager turned training-yard boss who runs the operation and speaks to pilots like they've got potential, even when they just pancaked into a rock.
    Function in game: menu-screen identity, tutorial/extraction/death callouts, phase-change commentary, leaderboard flavor, and the "voice" that makes runs feel less sterile.
    Personality pillars:
        Warm hardass
        Dry and unflappable
        Proud of competent flying
        Never cruel on failure
        Funny because he's observant, not because he's goofy
    Tone:
        Short lines
        Blue-collar space jargon
        Pithy jokes instead of long speeches
        Encouraging after failure
        Slightly competitive when the player is doing well
    Backstory:
        Slick has hauled wreckage, mined bad belts, lost ships, and watched hotshots flame out.
    What makes him memorable:
        He sounds like someone who has already survived every mistake the player is about to make.
        He praises smart flying like it matters.
        He needles overconfidence before disaster hits.
        He makes failure feel like part of learning rather than a dead end.
    Guardrails:
        No mean-spirited insults
        No wacky meme jokes
        No lore dumps
        No constant chatter that steps on tension
    Name recommendation:
        Keep "Slick" as the callsign/nickname. It's strong, readable, and already baked into the game's identity.
        If he needs a full name, use it rarely. Candidate: Silas "Slick" Mercer.
    Business name options:
        Slick's Salvage & Mining
        Slick's Salvage Yard
        Slick's Salvage School
        Slickline Salvage Training
    Recommended game-facing name:
        Slick's Salvage & Mining
